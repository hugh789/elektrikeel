Robert Hood Returns with New Track 'Art Form'

Detroit techno pioneer Robert Hood has released his first solo track of 2025, titled "Art Form," on his M-Plant label. This energetic and driving piece showcases Hood's signature minimalist style and is available now.

UK techno artist Surgeon is set to release his new album, "Shell~Wave," on May 2nd via Tresor. This nine-track LP reflects his deep connection with techno and follows his acclaimed 2023 album, "Crash Recoil".

Oscar Mulero Resumes Performances After Health Hiatus
